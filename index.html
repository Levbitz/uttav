    About us

    Career Uttsav is a 2-day annual Educational extravaganza held across multiple cities in India and the UAE. It provides an interactive platform to leading educational institutions to guide aspiring young students about various career options. The event is an excellent opportunity for students to engage with representatives from top institutions across the country and explore courses from different education streams. Psychometric assessments, Seminars and workshops and Mock entrance tests are the other highlights of this mega educational exposition.


    Our Vision

    Our vision is to equip students with clarity, conviction and determination to follow their dreams.


    Our Mission

    Our mission is to add value and create a collaborative ecosystem for all the stakeholders: students, parents, associates and partners.
