Past Events

Career Uttsav has had an amazing journey since its inception in 2013. The annual event has united institutions and students and put education and learning centre stage. The records speak for itself: with 600+ institution partnerships, it has reached out to more than 6,00,000 students across six locations in India and the UAE. Vice-Chancellors and heads of various institutions, eminent educationists and industrialists have graced the events and inspired thousands of students with their experience and wisdom.

The one-of-its kind mega educational fair has helped students to gain knowledge and clarity about various career paths. Students are exposed to a range of career exploration activities during the event. Opportunity to engage with representatives from top institutions, participation in interactive seminars and workshops, mock entrance tests and psychometric assessments have been some of the key takeaways for students attending Career Uttsav. Popular author and columnist, Chetan Bhagat; Motivational Speaker and Life coach, Gaur Gopal Das and Google Boy Kautilya Pandit and many other eminent speakers have motivated students during exclusive sessions that are a regular feature of Career Uttsav. The students also receive a complimentary copy of “Careers After +2”, that covers over 100 career options spread across different education streams.

Kota

Career Uttsav at the education hub of Rajasthan has received a great response from the strong student community. Specially curated interactive experiences, mock tests and workshops were part of the 2-day educational event at Kota.

Jaipur

The Pink City has played the perfect host to the mega educational event. Career Uttsav has been instrumental in creating awareness of the various career options that are available to students post their secondary education through its engaging activities.

Bangalore

The annual event has become a trusted brand with educational institutions, parents and students. The Garden City has witnessed record footfalls and participation from over 100+ top institutions across the country. Interactive experience zones, engaging workshops and mentorship sessions have proved to be a great learning experience for the participants.

Mangalore

The Career Uttsav at the port city of Mangalore gave students an opportunity to learn about a multitude of career options. The education fair has seen representation from big names like MVJ College of Engineering, SRM University, Oxford Group of Institutions, PES University, Don Bosco and Sambhram Group.

Hyderabad

Career Uttsav at Hyderabad was a huge draw among students. Participating universities had an opportunity to connect with students with their course offerings. Medical and engineering aspirants took up the mock tests to assess their entrance exam preparations. Students participated in mentoring sessions and availed one-to-one career counseling to gain an understanding of possible career options.
